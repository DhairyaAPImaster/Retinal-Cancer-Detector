How It Works

Upload your eye images and let our AI analyze them for potential issues. The model is trained on thousands of images and highlights risk with clear visual indicators. Results are displayed in easy-to-understand probability bars for each image.

Why We Made This

Many retinal issues go undetected in early stages. Our goal is to provide a quick, educational tool to help people notice potential risks early and consult professionals if needed. This app is not a diagnosis but a first step to awareness.
